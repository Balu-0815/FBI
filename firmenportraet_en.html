Company Profile

Welcome to FBI Bergmann Industrievertretungen
– Your Partner for Precision and Innovation

Since 1994, FBI Bergmann Industrievertretungen has stood for the reliable supply of tools, machinery, and systems for sheet metal processing. As an owner-managed family business, we focus on long-term partnerships, technical expertise, and uncompromising quality.

Our main focus is on:

  • Spare and wear parts for laser, plasma, waterjet, and oxy-fuel cutting systems – available from stock at short notice
  • System tools for punching and nibbling machines, press brake tools, and shear blades
  • Press brake tools and shear blades – custom-made on request
  • Regrinding and repair service for tools
  • Repair and overhaul of hose packages and torches for plasma systems

Whether standard solution or special production – we will find the right solution for your requirements.
Our experienced engineers will advise you personally and support your project from the idea to implementation.

What sets us apart:

  • Fast response times and individual advice
  • Flexible production and short delivery times
  • Many years of experience in sheet metal processing
  • Expertise, innovation, and reliability

Get to know us and our team – we look forward to your inquiry!
